Medications / Treatments to Disclose (Q2)

  • Blood thinners — Warfarin, Aspirin, Heparin
  • Retinoids / Acne medications — Accutane, Retinol, Tretinoin
  • Steroids — Prednisone, Hydrocortisone
  • Chemotherapy medications
  • Recent skin treatments — chemical peels, laser resurfacing, micro-needling, strong exfoliants

Allergies to Disclose (Q3)

  • Wax ingredients — resins, rosins, paraffin, beeswax, synthetic additives, fragrances
  • Pre/post-wax products — oils, lotions, aloe, gels, creams
  • Latex (gloves / applicators)
  • Previous skin reactions — contact dermatitis, irritation, redness

Health Conditions to Disclose (Q5)

  • Diabetes
  • Varicose veins
  • Circulatory / vascular issues
  • Skin infections — fungal, herpes simplex, warts, eczema, psoriasis
  • Immune system conditions
  • Recent surgeries or scars in treatment area
